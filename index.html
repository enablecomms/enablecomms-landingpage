Hi, I’m Tim Venables

Freelance Medical Writer

I am a freelance medical writer with 7 years of experience delivering high-quality content. I specialise in promotional writing and leveraging digital outputs to transform data into a narrative that audiences trust and act on. My work is built on a simple philosophy: scientific content is most effective when it has a purpose, coherence and influence. To achieve this, I use a three-part framework:

Strategic storylining

I apply a narrative that connects the evidence to the objective. This isn’t about “telling the story”, it’s a deliberate, structured process that defines the logic, flow, and hierarchy that ensures every section, slide, and sentence earns its place.

Collaborative calibration

Medical communications takes a village and the best work comes from alignment, not rework. I create clear, shared checkpoints with stakeholders, so decisions are made early and transparently. This reduces unnecessary amends and ensures everyone is confident in the project’s direction throughout.

Enabling action

My final outputs are designed not just to inform but to enable action. Through combining scientific accuracy with clear engaging storytelling, I can help teams bridge the gap between evidence and behaviour and ultimately deliver communications that make a meaningful impact.
